Our People

We’re passionate about creating a great place to work where our people feel valued, supported and encouraged to help shape a better future.

This means providing the technology, tools and workplaces that people need to do their job well. It’s also about providing professional development opportunities to help people to develop their skills and build their careers.

Our values underpin what we do and how we do it. They reflect our ambition and who we are, both as an organisation and as people.

  1. Innovation
    We believe in inspiring creativity, taking risks, feeling empowered and challenging the norm. We continuously learn and improve what we do and how we do it.
  2. Excellence
    We are always striving to be the best we can be. We focus on delivering efficient and effective solutions that meet and exceed our customer’s needs.
  3. Decency
    We value others, respect them, and treat everyone fairly and with dignity. We make the most of each other’s perspectives, knowledge and ideas.

Living out these values in the way we do business is key to our success. It means that BPDTS is an engaging and rewarding organisation to be part of and ensures DWP value the services and solutions we provide.
