Our Services

We’re a non-profit private limited company owned by the government. The DWP digital group are our sole customer.

We work with the DWP in agile ways and co-locate where possible supplying application, development maintenance and support services. This allows us to work closely together to monitor, fix and improve existing applications and introduce new up-to-date replacements and additions.

Our multi-disciplined team are technology experts who enjoy solving challenges and making a difference. We have software engineers specialising in VMC, Oracle and x as well as people working on cloud services, testing and database administration. The DWP is one of the largest and most complex integrated IT estates in Europe and we have many years’ experience modernising and maintaining the many applications that they use.

Our relationship with DWP, the skills of our people and the knowledge we’ve built up means we’re uniquely placed to deliver solutions that ensure:

  • people using DWP’s services get the support they need quickly and easily
  • DWP employees have more time to help the people who need their help most
  • we deliver services that are, and help DWP to be, more secure and cost efficient
