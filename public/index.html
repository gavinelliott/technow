Providing digital technology services to DWP

Benefits Pension Digital and Technology Services provide digital technology services to the Department for Work and Pensions

The DWP vision is to support the most vulnerable people in society and help them to transform their lives. We provide the digital technology that DWP needs to make this a reality.

We build and run secure and reliable digital technology that is used and valued by over 22 million people. Our technical expertise enable us to transform and run digital services efficiently and effectively.
